Global story about the product

Our approach is to utilize new technologies such as virtual reality, motion learning and real time tracking in order to create a new category of digital training & E-sports. We focus on motion learning, the real key of being able to play tennis well. In future, you will be able to learn, practice and play in virtual reality, as realistic and authentic as on the tennis court. There will be virtual tournaments and E-sport events & conventions bringing a whole new audience of folks and kids playing tennis.

What problem does this innovation solve?

Main goal of the VR Tennis Trainer platform is to help people learn to play tennis faster and cheaper as complementary tool along with training on court with an instructor. It will help to get up to speed learning basic movements and improve/perfect them with real-time computer assistance. The training is embedded in an entertaining and competitive game. The VR Tennis Trainer combines the playful fun of VR technology and effective tennis training sessions.

How to use this innovation

The player’s biomechanical data are scanned and processed while the system calculates the optimal pattern of movements. Then, the user puts on the VR goggles, takes the racket and starts playing tennis – on a virtual court, with virtual balls, instructed by a virtual coach and against virtual opponents. All body movements are accurately recorded and analyzed. Any deviations of the relevant parameters, like body position, legwork, arm position, stroke etc., are visualized on a “virtual dashboard” and via a real-time and replay “shadow”. At first, the player starts the training in slow motion, secondly progresses step by step in fast modus improving the strokes and body movements in order to learn perfect ones.

Who is this innovation for

This Tennis eSports product is for anyone who is willing to learn how to play tennis and wants to improve quickly and efficiently. Many tennis celebrities are already using this product (Andre Agassi, Andy Roddick, Boris Becker and many more). VR Tennis Trainer will help your learn new moves and anlalyze your real time performance after that you can apply your skills to a real time match since this machine is not a replacement for real time regular tennis excersize.

What can you expect from the service this company is delivering

Real-time motion tracking technologies, VR immersive environment and haptics devices are used to significantly improve the effect of immersion and motion learning. The platform immerses you into the world of virtual tennis where you can play as if you are on a real court. You will play with a real racket and feel the effect of ball hits even though the ball is a virtual one. You can move freely as if on real court. Core know-how of our platform is the motion learning methodology, i.e. set of technologies, tools, devices and software with proved learning paths. It guarantees fast and effective motion learning training. The methodology is based on the experience of professional trainers and sport biomechanics experts.
